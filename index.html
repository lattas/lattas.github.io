About me

I am researching how we can capture, generate and render realistic virtual humans with AI. I am currently a Researcher in Computer Vision at Huawei.

I have a PhD in Computer Science from Imperial College London, and have studied Computer Science and Innovation Management at Imperial College London and University College London

In the past, I spent some very interesting time at the Imperial spin-off Facesoft, Oracle and co-founded a youth entrepreneurship network in Greece.
